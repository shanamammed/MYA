Contribute to developing the media sector, invest your time in honing your capabilities and skills, and apply for the Young Arab Media Leaders Program
https://media.arabyouthcenter.org/
This is the largest media development program in which media organizations and experts participate, aiming to create the best generation of Arab youth in the media industry. The program seeks to hone the skills of Arab youth and provides them with the knowledge and experience to lead the Arab media industry. The program also helps young people to have access to the best media practices, and provides them with a learning opportunity in an integrated ecosystem that includes lessons, meetings and visits. Around 100 young men and women from all over the Arab countries participate in the program on an annual basis.
