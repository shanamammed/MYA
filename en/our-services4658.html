Convey your voice to decision makers by taking part in youth circles
https://circles.youth.gov.ae/
Youth Circles is a youth platform of discussions held regularly at different locations. On the platform, best practices and other major issues related to young people and their aspirations are discussed along with the challenges they face in order to come out with practical solutions, innovative
ideas and effective policies.

Youth Circles also gives young people the opportunity to express their opinions and impressions about their aspirations and challenges and collective participation in positive impact. Each youth circle tackles a certain topic and spells out solutions and ideas through practical discussions and recommendations from youth to youth.

26
Launch your business for free in the UAE markets
https://launchpad.youth.gov.ae/home
Youth Launchpad is an initiate by the Federal Youth Authority that provides young people with an opportunity to launch their businesses for free through launchpads specially created for them at the most vital areas in the UAE. The launchpads are equipped with basic services and connect them directly with customers. They give young people the ability to measure their performance while operating these spaces. They get a training opportunity to improve the quality of projects and develop their skills as entrepreneurs. Through the initiative,
opportunities are given to new projects every six months after assessment.

The ideas of having incubators within markets and other vital centers provides our young people with a great opportunity to compete with other major international projects and acquire entrepreneurial skills through practical experience.
